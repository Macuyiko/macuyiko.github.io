Edgy Tomorrow!

Ah the suspense… Edgy Eft is coming out tomorrow (if all goes well).

Here are the update instructions once more:

  1. (Backup.)
  2. gksu "update-manager -c -d" for the masses: quick and easy.
  3. Or replace dapper with edgy in /etc/apt/sources.list and do a sudo apt-get update && sudo apt-get dist-upgrade.
  4. Make sure everything is ok:
    sudo apt-get -f install
    sudo dpkg —configure -a Of course, this is all over Digg and others. But I just couldn’t help sharing this. It’ll be a tense day tomorrow…
